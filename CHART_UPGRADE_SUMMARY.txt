📊 DATA CALCULATIONS:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
✓ 24h High: Max of all candle highs
✓ 24h Low: Min of all candle lows
✓ Total Volume: Sum of all candle volumes
✓ Avg Volume: Total volume / candle count
✓ Distance to EMA: ((price - ema) / ema) × 100
✓ Distance to Levels: ((target - current) / current) × 100
