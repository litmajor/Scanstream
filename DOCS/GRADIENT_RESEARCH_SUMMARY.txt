WHAT GRADIENT STRENGTH ACTUALLY MEANS:

Direction:     Which way price is moving (up/down)
Strength:      How consistent/fast is the move (0-100%)
Acceleration:  Is the move speeding up or slowing down?

Example:
  Price: [100 → 101 → 102 → 103]
  ├─ Direction: BULLISH (all moves positive)
  ├─ Strength: 100% (4/4 candles bullish)
  └─ Acceleration: +0.5% (each candle slightly larger)

Versus:
  Price: [100 → 100.1 → 99.9 → 100.2 → 99.8]
  ├─ Direction: NEUTRAL (average close to zero)
  ├─ Strength: 20% (1/5 candles bullish)
  └─ Acceleration: RANDOM (no pattern)
  
REAL MARKET: Looks much more like the second example!
